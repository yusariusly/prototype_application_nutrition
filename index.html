Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my personalized nutrition program? expand_more
Once your practitioner finishes tailoring your diet plan, you will receive a notification. Simply log into the portal and navigate to the "Program" tab to see your dynamic calorie limits, macros, and daily recipe steps.
How do I book a consultation session? expand_more
Navigate to the "Appointments" tab, click the "Book New Session" button, select your desired consultation service, choose either Virtual (Online) or Clinic Visit (Offline), select date & time slots, and fill in your medical details.
Can I log custom meals or adjust calorie limits? expand_more
Your active goals and limits are customized by your nutritionist. You can log breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner achievements directly on your home Dashboard, or send an offline message on the program chat to request modification to your diet target from your dietitian.
How is my private medical/health data secured? expand_more
We value your confidentiality. All uploaded document files, session notes, progress scans, and private medical profiles are fully encrypted and only accessible by your primary assigned dietitian in compliance with digital health standards.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Sections 1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Data 3. Medical & Health Info 4. Security & Sharing 5. Your Data Rights

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide high-quality personalized dietary advice. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password, profile picture, and phone number when registering.
  • Health Profiles: Body weight record, fat ratio measurements, clinical history notes, meal logging achievements, food allergy lists, and goals.
  • Uploaded Material: Supporting documents, blood test reports, referrals, or dietitian instructions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To compile tailored macronutrient ratios, active calorie targets, and recommended meal schedules.
  • To host online telehealth video appointments and direct messages thread.
  • To keep track of dashboard analytics, water counters, weight curves, and performance progress.

3. Medical & Health Information Privacy

Health data is subject to strict standards. Your medical records, dietitian notes, and progress logs are only available to practitioners explicitly assigned to your profile in the client database.

We do not use clinical logs or dietary concerns data for marketing or advertisement profiling under any circumstances.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We implement industry-standard encryption practices to secure your data in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information. We only share details with:

  • Service providers facilitating virtual call infrastructures and file backup nodes.
  • Authorized medical practitioners for external clinical coaching referrals requested by you.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the absolute right to view, adjust, or delete your complete diagnostic profile and personal database records from our platform. If you wish to revoke permissions or permanently delete your account, please send a deletion request to support@nutriflow.health.
